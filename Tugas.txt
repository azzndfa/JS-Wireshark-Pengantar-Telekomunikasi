           = 20364785 bytes / 158,305 s
           = 128642,421 bytes
           = 1,029
Rata rata delay = Total delay / Total paket yang diterima
                = 158,305 sec / 25966
                = 0,0060 sec
                = 6 ms
Jitter = Total variasi delay / (Total paket diterima – 1)
       = 9 / 25965
       = 0,0003466 s
       = 0,3466 m

            = 6,407%
